# Shadows
A 2d platformer game where you are locked in a cave and trying to gain abilities and control your shadow to escape the cave. (the rest of the story is in part 2, coming soon..) 
<img width="545" height="322" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f3351f96-05b6-4c95-a3ef-86c353d7d1b7" />\
I made this game using godot engine and willing to put much effort to improve it until it's released on steam.
I started by coding the basic player movement and animations. Then created the tilemaps and designed the level. After this I created a collectable object called dark core which will be edited and used later to give more abilities. The dark core works by changing a bool in a global script that is responsible for allowinng the player to use abilities, like wall jump, double jump, dash, switch to shadow and more.
## Controls
WASD - arrows to move around, space to jump and q/shift to dash!
That's all the controls until now, there will be more abilities in this game with more controls so stay tuned for the next part.
Note: Some controls may not be mentioned here and the user will explore it in the game after gaining abilities.
## How to pass each level
For a better experience, do not read anything under this line before you give up anad don't know how to pass a specific level.
### Level 1
Collect the dark core, dash to pass to go through the white barrier
### Level 2
Dodge the saws and be careful of the cracked bricks.
### Level 3
Use the map to have a better vision that help is passing the level, and collect the red core to have the wall jump ability.
### Level 4
Use the map again to expand your vision and find a path out of the maze.
### Level 5
You will have to use the wall jump and dash abilities to pass this level.
### Level 6
Collect the new element "green core" that allows you to double jump once, collect it mid-air and jump until you reach the next one.
### Level 7
Die to your shadow 3 times to get the switch ability, allowing you to fly around and collect the 3 keys required to open the gate on the ceiling.
### Level 8
Switch to your shadow and try to collect the three keys and open the gate without touching the wall (note: dying will reset the key count).
### Level 9
Collect the first two keys, the third key will rise up once you touch it. You will notice the blocks above the key are different, so you should fly through them and get the key. And finally pick the last way in the bottom to reach the gate.
### Level 10
Collect the first two keys, the third key will be a trick too so you must go all the way back to get it and go back to the gate.
### Endless Level
Whenever you pass through an obstacle the score increases same as the spawn rate of the obstacles.
